Preparing Conant Lodge for the Next 100 Years:

We Need Your Participation!

2:03

The 100-year-old Conant Lodge is at the heart of the Cold River Camp experience that we cherish so much. The CRC Committee has approved the East Design to meet the future needs of Camp while maintaining an affordable outdoor experience for all. Groundbreaking is planned for autumn 2021. The Conant Lodge Campaign seeks your help now to raise the final one-third of the total $1.2 million needed for the project.

Many members of the CRC community have generously pledged leadership gifts ranging from $100 to $150,000, adding up to $850K pledged so far with $350K to go. We are illustrating our progress as a unique journey from Camp to the summit of S. Baldface. We have already passed through the woods and the Shelter and we are on the Ledges. We need you to help us pass the Knob and move on to the Summit!

Join with members of your community to invest in Cold River Camp’s next 100 years! Send a gift or pledge to donate by 2022. Click here for more details about how to make your gift.

Cold River Camp Update and Outlook for Summer 2021

Important News Summer 2021 Planning

Last September the Cold River Camp Committee established a Planning Committee, comprised of Camp Committee members, our regular season and Extension Managers, as well as volunteers with medical and emergency and contingency operations backgrounds, to develop plans for the summer of 2021 in view of COVID-19. We are examining carefully the Camp’s operations to assess whether we will be able to open Cold River Camp next summer. To develop a safe plan, the members are keeping three main points in mind: the safety and well-being of the guests and staff, the risk to the CRC guest experience, and the risk to the Cold River Camp brand and the longevity of the Camp. It is clear already that we will need to make changes to the way the Camp operates if we are to open next summer. Both the Cold Camp Committee and the AMC will vet any conclusion and recommendation that the Planning Committee reaches prior to announcing our final decision for the 2021 season. We will announce a final decision no later than February 15, 2021, or earlier, if possible.

Below is a highly preliminary schedule for the summer season. It will need to be reviewed and could be changed by the Camp 2021 Planning Committee and must be approved by the CRC Camp Committee. Please use this as a rough guide only and periodically check back for changes.

News Flash: CHANGE TO EARLY SUMMER SEASON REGISTRATION

The Cold River Camp Committee has agreed that, if Camp can open, it would be prudent to change the date for summer season full week reservations (normally the full month of January) to allow more time to assess the state of the pandemic and its effect on the experience at Camp. We want potential guests to know the details of the full plan, if we adopt a plan for opening in this COVID-19 time, before they register for Camp. We will periodically review and modify any plan developed depending on local and national guidelines throughout the spring and into the summer. We will post any changes on the Camp website and Camp community email blasts. FOR 2021 ONLY: Summer season full week reservations will be from April 1-10. Look for more information in January on this website and in the winter 2021 Little Deer Dispatch.

Hold the Presses: PUBLICATION OF THE WINTER LITTLE DEER DISPATCH (LDD) DELAYED UNTIL EARLY MARCH 2021

The LDD serves as a major way in which the Camp communicates with guests each January prior to Camp registration. The winter 2021 LDD will arrive via snail mail around the first week in March, well ahead of the April 1-10 registration window for full weeks. This issue will contain the most current information at the time on the operating plans for summer 2021, the Camp registration form, and some informative articles on various aspects of the Camp.
