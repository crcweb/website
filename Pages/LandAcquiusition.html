AMC Land Aquisition for CRC 1919 - 1986


Main Bungalow (c1918)
Conant Lodge (c2013)

The first plot of land was aquired for camp in 1919 by the Appalachian Mountain Club. It contained the Main Bungalow (now Conant Lodge), the Sisters Cabin (now Cox Cabin), the May Cottage (now the Library), and Lawrence Tower (now the Tower). An additiional 8 plots of land were acquirwd between 1920 and 1986.
