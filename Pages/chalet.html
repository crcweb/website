Chalet


Chalet from trail
Chalet from front

The Chalet is one of three cabins that sleeps only one person. For interior views of a typical family cabin go to CRC cabins

Chalet is equiped with one single bed with three blankets, mattress, pillow, linens and a set of towels. In addition, it has a closet, bureau, chair, bedside table, kerosene lamp, waste basket, fire extinguishe, smoke detector, fireplace, wood, and matches. A towel change is made part way through each week
