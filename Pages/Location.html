Cold River Camp Location

 

Cold River Camp of the Appalachian Mountain Club is located in North Chatham NH, along Rte. 113 in the Cold River Valley just south of Evans Notch. It is about a three to four hour drive from the Boston area, and about 17 miles north on Rte. 113 from Fryeburg ME.

Address for Camp: 32 AMC Rd., N. Chatham, NH, 03813

44.236421, -71.011823
