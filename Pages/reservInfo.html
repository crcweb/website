After careful research, the Summer Planning Committee has created a plan for safe access to Cold River Camp while the pandemic continues. The basics include reduced guest capacity, 3 homemade take-out meals/day, modifications to bathrooms along COVID guidelines, and hike consultants. We will keep abreast of the rapidly evolving AMC, CDC, and State of NH guidelines and adjust accordingly. 2021 Specials

Special Family Offer - Kids Stay at Cold River Camp 50%!

Weeks 1-9

We are resuming this special which has been popular in recent years to bring families new and old together. 2021 represents unique challenges to the camp. Instead of Kids Stay Free we are offering Kids Stay 50% off for all 9 weeks! This is a new promotion as of 5/1/21 and is retroactive to all previous reservations. For Weeks 1-9 any paying adult guest may bring up to two children under the age of 12 50% off. This offer is good for all Cold River Camp guests. Offer is limited to one full week only per family, cannot be be used with a partial week.
