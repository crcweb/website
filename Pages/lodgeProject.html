The Conant Lodge Project

Status update 6/16/2021

The Conant Lodge Renovation Committee Sally Morris, CRC Committee Chair, Jan Irvin, Mark Winkler, Frank Mastro, CRC Facilities Manager, Cheryl Poirier), is working carefully with Garland Mill to complete the final details of the design for the rehabilitation of the Kitchen into a new Dining Room and the design for the all new Kitchen addition. Garland is scheduled to begin work on the project on September 7, 2021.  

The Conant Lodge Campaign Committee (Sally Morris, CRC Camp Committee Chair, Gary Munson, Angela Liptack, Lee Heald, Ann Prindle, and Kathy Roth Huse) has through generous gifts from CRC members raised more than $900,000 in pledges toward the $1.2 million needed for this project, yet COVID has caused materials to be scarce. We aim now for $1.4 to ensure we have enough. We are submitting a grant application to LCHIP (https://lchip.org/). The Campaign Committee now invites all the CRC community and beyond to get involved in order to meet the goal.
