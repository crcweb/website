COLD RIVER CAMP
Group Rental

For group reservations mid-September through mid-October, mid-May through mid-June:
• Cost - $65 per person per night with a 15 person minimum
• Deposit - $200 non-refundable.
• Two nights minimum stay

CRC provides:
• All meals - Dinner, Breakfast, and Trail Lunch
• Camp Representative on-site
• Firewood for cabin fireplaces and stoves
• Limited wifi access

Group provides:
• Personal linens
• 2 volunteers for groups 15 to 20 or 4 volunteers for groups over 20 to help serve meals (cafeteria style) and clean up after meals
• All guests are responsible for sweeping cabins, folding blankets and removing trash from cabins at the end of their stay
