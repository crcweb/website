Come experience the "Off Season" at Cold River Camp! Off Season runs mid-May through mid-June and mid-September through mid-October. Camp is a quieter place. The spring offers returning birds at their most colorful and frolicsome, flowers abloom, and full-running streams and cascades.  The fall offers stunning foliage and the nearby renowned Fryeburg Fair. Throughout, enjoy hiking, biking or paddling in cooler weather, and savor crisp, starry nights. Advance registration required. Additional details available from Off-Season Manager (see contact information below).
