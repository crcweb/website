Ledgeview Family Cabin


Ledgeview family cabin from trail
Ledgeview from back

Ledgeview is a family cabin. It sleeps four. It is divided into two rooms of two beds each but only one of the rooms has a fireplace. For interior views of a typical family cabin go to CRC cabin.

Ledgeview is equiped with four single beds each with three blankets, mattress, pillow, linens and a set of towels for each person. In addition, it has a closet, bureau, chairs, bedside table, kerosene lamp, waste basket, fire extinguisher, smoke detector, fireplace, wood, and matches. A towel change is made part way through each week
