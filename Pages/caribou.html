East and West Caribou Family Cabin


Inside East Caribou family cabin
Stove and view into West Caribou side

East and West Caribou are duplex units in a family cabin. Each unit sleeps two. It is ideal for a family of 4 persons or two families of 2 or less each. Caribou Cabin is the most recent addition to the CRC family cabins. It was constructed in 2012 - 2013 and finished just in time for the 2013 full service season. Pictures of the dedication and construction with interior views are available.

Both East and West Caribou units are equipped with two single beds each with three blankets, mattress, pillow, linens and a set of towels for each person. In addition, each unit has a closet, bureau, chairs, bedside table, kerosene lamp, waste basket, fire extinguisher, and smoke detector. Only the East room has a wood stove with matches and tools and matches. A towel change is made part way through each week
