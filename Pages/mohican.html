Mohican Ravine Cabin


Mohican Ravine Cabin from trail
Deck and view from Mohican Ravine Cabin


Mohican has a deck providing views down into the ravine and to the mountains north, east, and west of camp.

Mohican is equiped with two single beds each with three blankets, mattress, pillow, linens and a set of towels for each person. In addition, the cabin has a closet, bureau, two chairs, bedside table, kerosene lamp, waste basket, fire extinguisher, smoke detector, fireplace, wood, and matches. A towel change is made part way through each week. It is available only for adults.

For interior views of a typical ravine cabin go to CRC cabins.
