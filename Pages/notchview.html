Notchview Ravine Cabin


Looking over Notchview Ravine Cabin towards the mountain.
Interior view of Notchview Ravine Cabine.


The new Notchview Ravine Cabin was finished and landscaped in June 2009.

Notchview is equiped with two single beds each with three blankets, mattress, pillow, linens and a set of towels for each guest. In addition, the cabin has a closet, bureau, two chairs, bedside table, kerosene lamp, waste basket, fire extinguisher, smoke detector, wood stove, wood, and matches. A towel change is made part way through each week. Ravine cabins are only available for adults.
