About Bird Camp
Birding enthusiasts from around the region will meet up at AMC's Cold River Camp (www.amccoldrivercamp.org) for a weekend of birding during peak migration in the wild & varied habitats around Chatham, NH.  Starts with Thursday evening dinner & ends after Sunday breakfast; come for 2 or 3 nights. Includes scheduled outings & evening programs in an informal and comfortable atmosphere.  Just $89/night for lodging & all meals.  À la carte options for meals & trips available. All ages & experience levels welcome.
