Barracks


Barracks from the front
Beds in the bunkroom


The Barracks has an efficient wood stove in the bunkroom to provide heat if needed. In the bunkroom, there are six single beds with three blankets per bed, mattress, pillow, linens and a set of towels. In addition, the bunkroom has a small closet and chairs for each guest, a bedside table, waste basket, fire extinguisher, smoke detector, wood and matches. A towel change is made part way through each week.

The Barracks has electricity and two small separate rooms with running water, one with a sink and the other with a sink and toilet.
